About Us

Macro Lillypad is a pre-construction condo complex project with home automation. Our goal is provide affordable housing thats suits anyones needs wether you are paying off a bank loan or simply earn a low income. Macro Lillypad also provides you with a simple yet detailed payment plan throughout your stay. From self operating doors to automated kitchen equipmet, these condos are completely controled by a swip of a hand or simply the sound of your voice.

Preferences and Values

The condos are specialy designed to suit young modern couples that love to explore new technology and adapt easily to a new modern way of living like parking your car in a self operating elevator or a ready made coffee waiting for you on a cold winter day.
